Karate Terms - Instructions

- YAME (ya-may)
  - Return to starting position (typically hachiji-dachi)

- HAJIME (hajee-may)
  - Begin

KAMAE: Get into position, prepare.

MOKUSO: Close the eyes and meditate.

MATTE (ma-tay): Wait.
